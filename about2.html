I am interested in multisensory perception and distractions across the lifespan. In my PhD I used a combination of psychophysics and EEG to investigate cross-modal (audio-visual) distractibility. Currently I am a postdoc at Trinity College Dublin, where I work with The Irish Longitudinal Study on Ageing (TILDA) to investigate multisensory judgements in a large group of adults aged over 50 years.

What is a science officer? I am just learning myself to be honest. I love creating and coding experiments and I have always coded my own experiments in python using PsychoPy. In September 2020 I joined the PsychoPy team to help other scientists create their experiments. My role involves education and outreach including running workshops , offering consultancy services and helping scientists launch their tasks online.
